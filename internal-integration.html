How to connect EkaScribe with EkaDoc

Note: This integration requires an active EkaScribe subscription and EkaDoc account.

You'll need to authorize EkaScribe to access your EkaDoc records during setup.

1 Enable EkaDoc integration in EkaScribe

  1. Log in to your EkaScribe dashboard
  2. Navigate to Settings → Integrations
  3. Find EkaDoc in the list of available integrations
  4. Click Connect to begin the authorization process

2 Authorize your EkaDoc account

  1. You'll be redirected to EkaDoc's secure authorization page
  2. Log in with your EkaDoc credentials
  3. Review the permissions EkaScribe is requesting
  4. Click Authorize to grant access
  5. You'll be redirected back to EkaScribe

3 Configure your sync preferences

  1. Choose which types of notes to sync automatically (SOAP notes, prescriptions, etc.)
  2. Set your preferred file format and naming conventions
  3. Select notification preferences for successful syncs
  4. Click Save Settings to complete the setup
That's it! Your EkaScribe and EkaDoc accounts are now connected. All notes will automatically sync to your patient records.
