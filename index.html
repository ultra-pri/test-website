Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about EkaScribe

What is EkaScribe?

EkaScribe is an AI-powered medical scribe that records doctor–patient conversations and automatically generates summaries, clinical notes, and custom structured notes with clinical-grade accuracy.

Is patient data secure?

Yes. EkaScribe is built with the highest standards of security and privacy, adhering to HIPAA and ISO guidelines. All audio and text data is fully encrypted both in transit and at rest to ensure complete protection.

How much time can be saved?

On average, our users report saving over 12 hours per week. By automating clinical documentation, EkaScribe reduces administrative work by up to 80%, giving doctors more time to focus on patient care.

Which languages are supported?

EkaScribe supports more than 20 languages, including English, Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Tamil, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Urdu, and many more. It is optimised for medical accents and terminology to ensure accuracy.

How does EMR integration work?

Our Chrome extension enables one-click insertion of AI-generated notes into any web-based EMR system. For offline EMR systems, you can easily copy the structured text to your clipboard and paste it directly.

Are there limits on consultations?

In the Free plan, there is a limit of 5 consultations per day. In the Pro plan, there are no limits on the number of consultations you can use EkaScribe for. To prevent misuse, we have certain safeguards in place, but these never affect genuine doctors or real clinical usage.

Is the Pro plan for a single doctor or multiple doctors?

The Pro plan is intended for individual doctors. If you need licenses for multiple doctors, please reach out to our team through the Contact Us option on the pricing page, and we'll be happy to assist.
